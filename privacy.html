Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to facilitate the buying and selling of books. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email address, Phone number, and City.
  • Listing Information: Book titles, photos, prices, and descriptions you upload.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase (by Google) to securely store your data and manage logins.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of connecting buyers and sellers:

  • Public Visibility: Your name and city are visible on your book listings.
  • Contacting Sellers: Your phone number is not publicly displayed on the website. It is only used to generate a WhatsApp link so interested buyers can contact you directly.

3. Data Security

We rely on Google Firebase's secure infrastructure to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and database hosting.
  • WhatsApp: We redirect users to WhatsApp for messaging. We do not read or store your WhatsApp messages.
